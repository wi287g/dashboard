About the data

287(g) — Sheriff/police agencies that signed a Memorandum of Agreement (MOA) with ICE to perform immigration enforcement functions from local jails or in the field.

Detainers — Civil requests from ICE asking a jurisdiction to hold someone beyond their lawful release. Honoring detainers without 287(g) MOAs is noted separately.

SCAAP — State Criminal Alien Assistance Program payments: federal reimbursements to localities that incarcerated people with certain immigration statuses.

Data is best-effort and may lag real-time changes. Sources include public ICE 287(g) lists, ACLU-WI reports, open-records responses, and federal BJS SCAAP award data. Unknown or missing values are shown explicitly. See README for methodology.
